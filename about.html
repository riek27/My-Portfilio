About IM STORE

Technology that Connects You to the Future

Who We Are

IM STORE is a premier technology and internet solutions provider based in Juba, South Sudan. We specialize in delivering cutting-edge connectivity solutions to businesses and individuals across the country.

Founded with a vision to bridge the digital divide in South Sudan, we provide reliable internet access through advanced technologies like Starlink satellite systems, along with professional networking equipment from industry leaders such as Mikrotik and Ruijie.

Our team of certified technicians and network specialists is dedicated to ensuring that our clients receive the best possible service, from initial consultation to installation and ongoing support.

Our Mission

To empower communities and businesses in South Sudan with reliable, high-speed internet connectivity and cutting-edge technology solutions that drive growth, innovation, and development.

Our Vision

To become the leading provider of technology solutions in South Sudan, connecting every community to the digital world and enabling a future where technology serves as a catalyst for progress.
